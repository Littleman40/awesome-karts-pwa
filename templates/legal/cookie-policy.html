Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and maintain your session between page visits.

How We Use Cookies

Awesome Karts uses session cookies to keep you logged in during your visit. These are strictly necessary for the booking system to function and are deleted when you close your browser or log out. We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

Our payment provider Stripe may set cookies on your device when you proceed to checkout. These are governed by Stripe's own privacy and cookie policies. We do not control or have access to these cookies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling session cookies will prevent you from logging in or completing a booking. For instructions, refer to your browser's help documentation.

Contact

For cookie-related enquiries, contact us at hello@awesomekarts.com.au.
