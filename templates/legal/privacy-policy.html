Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Introduction

Awesome Karts Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the NSW Privacy and Personal Information Protection Act 1998.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information you provide when creating an account or making a booking, including your name, date of birth, email address, phone number, postal address, and payment information. Payment data is processed securely by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to process bookings, send booking confirmations and reminders, manage waivers, and provide customer support. We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except where required to deliver our services (e.g., payment processing via Stripe, SMS delivery via Twilio) or where required by law.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage of passwords and secure HTTPS connections. We follow ISO/IEC 27001 best practices where applicable.

Access and Correction

You have the right to access and correct your personal information at any time via your account dashboard or by contacting us directly.

Contact

For privacy enquiries, contact us at hello@awesomekarts.com.au.
